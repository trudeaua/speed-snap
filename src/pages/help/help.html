Help Speed Snap Tips & Tricks
  • Swipe right on a survey on the home page to delete it
  • Press and hold survey items on the 'Review Your Photos' page to edit/remove them
  • Tap the     icon to open app settings
    Here you can change your contact information that is displayed on generated PDF's and default units for measuring signs.

  • Every time you add a survey item to the session log, your survey is saved!
    That way you can easily navigate between surveys without worrying about data loss.
